About

Hi, I’m Scott. I’m based in the UK and spend a good chunk of my free time tinkering with things I probably should’ve left alone. I enjoy building small projects, learning how systems work (usually by breaking them first), and occasionally finishing something before immediately starting the next idea.

When I’m not doing that, I’m usually gaming (World of Warcraft, Path of Exile), taking long night drives with the music far too loud, or disappearing down Wikipedia rabbit holes when I should be asleep.

Musically, I lean towards the emotionally heavy end of the spectrum. Favourites include Deaf Havana, Nothing More, Architects, and Sleep Token — essentially anything that sounds like it’s having a slight existential crisis.

This site is a home for personal projects, interests, and the odd experiment. Nothing too serious, nothing overly polished, but everything here exists for a reason. Probably.

If you’ve made it this far, feel free to have a look around — or don’t. I’m not your dad.
